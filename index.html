Events by Department

The visualization shows all the Earthquakes by department that had more than 7M of intensity since there was a record, in color gray for the time of the Colony and Republic and in green with the sismometers. The Moment Magnitude (M) which is the intensity of the event is represented by the area of the circle.

Where are the events located?

The visualization shows all the Earthquakes that had more than 5.5M of intensity since 1960, labelling the more intensive ones. The variables describe in the visualization are the Moment Magnitude (M) which is the intensity of the event and it is represented by the area of the circle and the Depth of the event represented by the color inside the circle.

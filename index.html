Earthquakes in Perú

The visualization shows all the Earthquakes that had more than 5.5M of intensity since 1960 and labelling the ones greater than 7.8M. The intensity scale reached by an earthquake is called Moment Magnitude (M) and it is represented by the area of the circle. The color which is lighter or darker represents how deep was the event.
The destructive power between consecutive scales is 31.6 times more. Say an earthqueake of 7M is 31.6 more powerfull than a 6M. An 8M earthquake is 31.6 x 31.6 ≈ 1,000 times more powerful than a 6M!

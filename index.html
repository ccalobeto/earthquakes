Peru Earthquakes since 1960

The visualization shows all the Earthquakes that had more than 5.5M of intensity since 1960. The variables describe in the visualization are the Moment Magnitude (M) which is the intensity of the event represented by the area of the circle and the Depth of the event represented by the color inside the circle.
